ABOUT US

We are an organisation that aims to be the leading technological hub for girls and women in Africa. We believe that women and girls hold the key to the much needed technological revolution that Africa needs; and as such, we are providing a platform for women and girls from all over Africa to learn how to code so that when the opportunity arises, they can be at the helm of technological affairs driving the continent and in turn: the globe.
